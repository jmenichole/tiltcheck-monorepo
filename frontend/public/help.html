Quick Answers

How do I sign up?

Click the "Login" button at the top-right and connect with your Discord account. Your account is created automatically.

What happens to my data?

We only store your Discord username and avatar. We never access your passwords or personal messages. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I report a scam or problem?

Use the Contact form to report issues, or reach out in our Discord community.

Is TiltCheck free?

Yes! All core features are free. Most tools and trust scoring are available to everyone. Some premium features may be added later.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Contact support with your request and we'll delete your data within 30 days.

Do you have a mobile app?

We offer a PWA (Progressive Web App) that works on mobile. Click "Install PWA" on the dashboard or use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option.

Still need help?

Our community is here to support you. Reach out via contact form, Discord, or check our documentation.
