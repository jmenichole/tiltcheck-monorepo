Frequently Asked Questions

What does beta testing involve?

As a beta tester, you'll get early access to new features before public release. We ask that you use the tools, report bugs, and share your honest feedback through our in-app widget or Discord.

Is there any cost to join?

No! Beta access is completely free. We're grateful for your time and feedback, which is why we offer rewards and perks to active testers.

How do I provide feedback?

We've built a feedback widget right into our tools. You can also share feedback in our Discord beta channel, or schedule video calls with the team for detailed discussions.

What data do you collect?

We collect anonymous usage data and click heatmaps to improve the product. All data is processed internally - we never share with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I invite friends?

Yes! We have a referral program. Share your unique link and both you and your friend get bonus rewards when they join.
