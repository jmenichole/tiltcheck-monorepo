Degen Trust Engine

Accountability without shame. Transparency without punishment.

Trust, but make it degen-friendly.

The Degen Trust Engine is TiltCheck’s reputation system — and unlike every other “trust score” in existence, it’s actually fair.

It doesn’t guess. It doesn’t judge. It doesn’t punish you forever because you tilted once at 3AM. It recovers automatically and adapts to human behavior.

This isn’t a morality engine. This is a realistic model of degen patterns, built by an actual degen who understands how chaotic the game can be.

Why This Exists

Because typical trust systems are toxic, permanent, punitive, biased, and easily abused.

And degens don’t need a parole officer — they need a system that understands mood swings, tilt, generosity, accountability, and good faith.

TiltCheck gives users a score that actually reflects how they behave — not how a casino or mod feels about them.

How It Works

  • Tilt Indicators (Temporary)
    Spike when a user rages, spams, overshoots bets, or gets unstable. Recover automatically over time. This is the “mood meter,” not a character judgment.
  • Behavior Score
    General conduct in chats, games, interactions.
  • Scam Flags
    -15 for verified scam behavior. False reports penalize both sides.
  • Accountability Tools Usage
    Cooldowns, smart withdrawals, vault locks, phone-a-friend. Using these tools increases trust.
  • Community Reports
    Weighted for accuracy. “I don’t like them” = ignored. “Stole 100 USDC and ghosted” = logged.

Trust Levels

  • VERY HIGH (95–100)
    Model citizen, smart choices, no tilt storms.
  • HIGH (80–94)
    Normal chaos but handled responsibly.
  • NEUTRAL (60–79)
    Average degen. Erratic but not harmful.
  • LOW (40–59)
    Some issues. Needs accountability tools.
  • HIGH RISK (<40)
    Scams, ghosting, repeated chaos, volatility.

Event-Driven Scoring

  • tip.completed → generosity boost
  • tilt.detected → temporary penalty
  • cooldown.violated → behavior penalty
  • scam.reported → -15 if verified
  • accountability.success → trust bonus
  • vault.locked → responsibility bonus
  • collectclock.failure → tilt correlation

Every event is logged. Every score is explainable. Every change publishes.

Natural Recovery (The MVP Feature)

TiltCheck understands that degens are human. Tilt indicators decay by 0.5/hour, automatically. No one is punished forever. No grudges. No “permanent red score.” You tilt → you calm → you return to normal. Simple, human, fair.

What the Score Actually Tells Communities

  • scammers
  • soft scammers
  • good-faith degens
  • stable bettors
  • tilt-prone users
  • high-accountability players
  • users who use tools responsibly
  • users who spike in chaos during bad runs

It’s not a punishment system — it’s a safety system.

Stored Locally & Transparent

No hidden data. No black box. No secret moderator controls. Everything is explainable and auditable.
