# 1. TiltCheck Brand & Identity

TiltCheck isn’t a corporate tool.  
It’s a **community-born degen intelligence system** built by someone who actually understands the problems casinos, bots, bonus cycles, and users create.

The brand is intentionally:

- direct  
- funny  
- honest  
- helpful  
- smart  
- and zero-bullshit  

TiltCheck is the opposite of casino marketing fluff.  
It tells the truth, calls out shady behavior, and gives users tools to protect themselves (and each other) without killing the fun.

---

# 1.1 Brand Pillars

### **1. Degen Empathy**
TiltCheck doesn’t preach.  
It doesn’t moralize.  
It doesn’t try to stop people from gambling.

It’s built by someone who actually understands:

- bonus cycles  
- rage bets  
- tilt  
- payout delays  
- shady promos  
- predatory loyalty programs  
- math mismatches  
- "pump and dump" fairness seeds  

TiltCheck is honest because it has lived the same experiences.

---

### **2. Transparency > Hype**
Casinos love opacity.  
TiltCheck hates it.

The brand values:

- fairness  
- clarity  
- evidence  
- user empowerment  

When a casino is shady, TiltCheck calls it out.  
When users behave recklessly, TiltCheck nudges — with humor, not shame.

---

### **3. Fun Is Mandatory**
TiltCheck is serious about infrastructure but playful in tone.

Humor is part of the brand DNA.

Where appropriate:

- sarcastic asides  
- clever one-liners  
- light trolling  
- degen memes  
- “I know you just rage-spun 50x tougher than the slot did”  

But never cruel.  
Never judgmental.  
Always community-first.

---

### **4. Non-Custodial Forever**
TiltCheck will **never** hold or control user funds.  
The brand loudly supports:

- user-owned wallets  
- transparent swaps  
- no-custody tipping  
- crystal-clear fee structure  

The ethos:  
> “Your crypto stays yours. We don’t babysit it.”

---

### **5. Built by a Solo Founder**
TiltCheck is proud to be founder-built.  
Not a committee.  
Not a VC-backed, over-engineered corporate project.

Just:

- a builder  
- a problem-solver  
- a degen  
- and someone who loves making tools work better  

The tone reflects this authenticity.

---

# 1.2 The TiltCheck Tagline

**“Made for degens, by degens.”**

This appears in footers, not headlines.  
It’s part of the culture, not the marketing.

# 1.4 Visual Identity (High-Level)

TiltCheck favors:

- minimalism  
- clean lines  
- dark/light mode friendly colors  
- purple and green accents  
- readable typography  
- “dashboard” vibes  
- non-glossy UI  

Everything should feel:

- trustworthy  
- simple  
- zero-to-tilt in 0.02 seconds  

The visuals should complement the tone:  
clean, practical, unpretentious.

---

# 1.5 What TiltCheck is NOT

- Not moralistic  
- Not corporate  
- Not casino-aligned  
- Not compliance theater  
- Not an overbuilt SaaS monstrosity  
- Not influencer-driven  
- Not a hype token or rug  
- Not a gambling addiction program  

TiltCheck exists because degens deserve safer, smarter tools *without being talked down to.*
