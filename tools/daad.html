What is it?

TL;DR: Cards Against Humanity for Discord, but crypto-themed. Submit cards, vote for the funniest, win points. Separate bot so it doesn't clutter your main server.

DA&D is a fill-the-blank card game where one player's prompt card meets everyone else's answer cards. Judge picks the most unhinged response. Rotating roles, anonymous voting, and degen-themed card packs. Perfect for Discord communities that appreciate dark humor.

How It Works - Complete Guide

1

Game Setup (Host Creates Game)

Any player can create a game in a Discord channel. Others can join until the host starts the game.

  • Command: /play to create, /join for others
  • Players: 2-10 players required
  • Card Pack: Starts with "Degen Starter Pack" (crypto casino themed)
  • Dealing: Each player gets 7 white cards (answers)
2

Round Begins (Prompt Revealed)

One player is the judge for each round. A black card (prompt with blanks) is revealed to all players.

  • Black Card Example: "When I'm tilting, I _ instead of touching grass."
  • Judge: Rotates each round so everyone gets a turn
  • Non-judges: Must submit one white card from their hand
  • Privacy: Submissions are anonymous until voting
3

Players Submit Cards

Each player (except the judge) selects one white card from their hand that best completes the prompt.

  • Your Hand: Use /hand to see your cards in a DM
  • Submit: /submit card:3 to play card #3 from your hand
  • Timer: 60 seconds to submit (auto-submit random card if timeout)
  • Anonymous: Judge won't know who submitted which card
4

Voting & Scoring

All submitted cards are revealed anonymously. The judge picks the funniest/best answer. Winner gets a point.

  • Judge Picks: /vote player:@username to award point
  • Scoring: 1 point per round won
  • Leaderboard: /scores shows current standings
  • Next Round: Judge role rotates, everyone draws back to 7 cards
5

Game Continues Until Complete

Play continues through multiple rounds until someone reaches the winning score or the game is manually ended.

  • Win Condition: First to 7 points (configurable)
  • Rounds: Each player judges at least once before game ends
  • Final Scores: Winner announced, full leaderboard displayed
  • Stats: Cards played, rounds won, participation tracked

Example Round

Black Card: "I lost $10K on a _ play. Never touching leverage again."

Submissions (anonymous):

  • 1. "100x short on a meme coin"
  • 2. "midnight drunk trading"
  • 3. "my wife's college fund"
  • 4. "revenge trading after a bad beat"

Judge picks: #3 wins the round. That player gets 1 point.

Why this works: Cards Against Humanity gameplay meets crypto degen culture. The "Degen Starter Pack" includes 100+ cards themed around casino gambling, crypto trading, and the absolute degeneracy of Discord financial communities.

Why a Separate Bot?

DA&D runs on its own dedicated Discord bot instead of being part of the main TiltCheck bot. Here's why:

  • Less Clutter: Game commands don't mix with earning/safety commands
  • Different Audiences: Some servers just want games, some just want tips/promos
  • Performance: Game state management doesn't slow down tipping transactions
  • Permissions: Game bot can have different permission levels than financial bot

Invite whichever bots your community wants. No forced bundles.
