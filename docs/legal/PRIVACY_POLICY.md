## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Discord User Data
When you interact with TiltCheck, we collect:

- **Discord User ID** (unique identifier)
- **Discord Username** and discriminator
- **Server/Guild ID** where commands are used
- **Message content** (only for commands, link scanning, and tilt detection)
- **Timestamps** of interactions

### 2.2 Wallet Information
If you use JustTheTip or wallet-related features:

- **Solana wallet addresses** (external, user-provided)
- **Transaction signatures** (on-chain, publicly visible)

**We do NOT collect:**
- Private keys
- Seed phrases
- Custody of any funds

### 2.3 Gameplay & Casino Data
When using TiltCheck analytics:

- **Casino names** and session data
- **Gameplay patterns** (bet sizes, win/loss ratios)
- **Tilt detection signals** (message frequency, rage patterns)
- **Bonus and promo submissions**

### 2.4 Technical Data
We automatically collect:

- **Command usage logs** (which commands, when used)
- **Error logs** (for debugging and improvement)
- **IP addresses** (temporarily, for rate limiting only)

### 2.5 AI Processing
Some data is processed by third-party AI services (OpenAI, Vercel AI) for:
- Natural language understanding
- Tilt detection analysis
- Casino trust scoring

These providers have their own privacy policies.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

- **Provide TiltCheck services** (tipping, scanning, trust scoring)
- **Improve functionality** (detect bugs, optimize performance)
- **Detect fraud & abuse** (prevent scams, spam, exploitation)
- **Generate trust scores** (casino and user reputation)
- **Personalize experiences** (track preferences, onboarding progress)
- **Communicate with you** (DMs for setup, notifications)

We **DO NOT:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for unrelated advertising
- Share personally identifiable information without consent (except as required by law)

---

## 4. Data Storage & Retention

### 4.1 Storage Location
Data is stored in:
- **JSON files** (local persistence for wallets, preferences, sessions)
- **Supabase** (free-tier database for trust scores, promo submissions)
- **Discord servers** (message history as per Discord's retention policy)

### 4.2 Retention Period
We retain data:
- **Active user data:** Indefinitely while you use TiltCheck
- **Onboarding progress:** Until you opt out or delete your data
- **Transaction logs:** 90 days (for fraud prevention)
- **Error logs:** 30 days
- **Inactive accounts:** Data may be purged after 1 year of inactivity

---

## 5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share data with:

### 5.1 Discord
- TiltCheck operates within Discord's infrastructure
- Discord has access to all messages and interactions per their privacy policy

### 5.2 Blockchain Networks
- All Solana transactions are **publicly visible** on-chain
- Wallet addresses and transaction signatures are permanently recorded

### 5.3 AI Providers
- OpenAI (GPT models for AI conversations and analysis)
- Vercel AI (AI Gateway for processing)
- Data is anonymized where possible

### 5.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose data if required by:
- Court orders or legal processes
- Law enforcement requests
- Protection of TiltCheck rights and safety
