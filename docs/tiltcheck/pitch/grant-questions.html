Sample Grant Application Questions

Use these questions to prepare your grant application for TiltCheck funding opportunities.

General Information

  • What is the name of your organization?
  • Provide a brief description of your project.
  • What is the mission and vision of your organization?

Project Details

  • What problem does your project aim to solve?
  • Describe the target audience for your project.
  • What are the key milestones and deliverables?

Budget and Funding

  • What is the total budget for your project?
  • How will the grant funds be allocated?
  • Do you have other sources of funding? If so, please list them.

Impact and Evaluation

  • What are the expected outcomes of your project?
  • How will you measure the success of your project?
  • What is the long-term impact of your project?
