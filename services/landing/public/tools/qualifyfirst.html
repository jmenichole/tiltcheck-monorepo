How It Works - Complete Guide

1

Build Your Profile Once

Answer questions about demographics, interests, and lifestyle. The more complete your profile, the better AI can match you.

  • Demographics: Age range, location, household income
  • Lifestyle: Pet ownership, car, children, employment
  • Interests: Hobbies, shopping habits, tech comfort
  • Privacy: All data stays in your profile - you control what's shared
2

AI Matches You With Surveys

Our matching algorithm analyzes your profile traits against survey requirements and historical screen-out data.

  • High Confidence (75%+): Strong profile match + low screen-out history
  • Medium Confidence (40-74%): Partial match or unknown territory
  • Low Confidence (<40%): Weak match or high screen-out risk
  • AI Reasoning: See why each survey was recommended
3

Complete Surveys & Earn

Take surveys matched to your profile. Earnings accumulate automatically as you complete surveys.

  • Real-time Earnings: Track total earned, completion rate, average per survey
  • Screen-out Learning: System remembers which surveys screened you out
  • Avoidance: Future matches automatically filter out similar surveys
  • Profile Improvement: Get recommendations for new traits to add
4

Withdraw Anytime (Non-Custodial)

Request withdrawals through JustTheTip's non-custodial system. Funds go directly to YOUR wallet.

Why This Matters

Traditional survey sites waste your time. You spend 5-10 minutes answering pre-qualification questions, then get screened out with nothing. That's time you'll never get back.

QualifyFirst fixes this. By building your profile once and using AI to predict survey matches, you avoid wasting time on surveys you'll never qualify for. Higher completion rate = more money per hour spent.

Non-custodial withdrawals mean you're always in control. Your earnings are yours. We can't freeze them, withhold them, or steal them. If TiltCheck disappeared tomorrow, you'd still have full access to your funds.
