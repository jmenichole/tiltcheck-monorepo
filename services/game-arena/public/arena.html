Step 1: How to Play

Degens Against Decency is a hilarious party game where players match answer cards to fill in the blank on question cards. The Card Czar picks the funniest combo!

  • Each round, one player is the Card Czar
  • Other players submit their funniest answer cards
  • The Card Czar picks the winner
  • First to reach the point goal wins!

Step 2: Creating a Game

You can create your own game room or join an existing one from the lobby below.

  • Click "Create Game" to start your own room
  • Set the max players (3-7 recommended)
  • Choose public or private room
  • Share the game link with friends!

Step 3: Bot Players

Don't have enough players? No problem! Our AI bots will automatically join to ensure you have the minimum 3 players needed.

  • Bots join automatically when you start a game with less than 3 players
  • They play just like real players
  • Bots leave when real players join
  • Perfect for solo practice or testing!
