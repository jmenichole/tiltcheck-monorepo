## Presentation Tips

### Before Presenting:
- [ ] Practice timing (aim for 5-10 minutes for full deck)
- [ ] Prepare for Q&A
- [ ] Test technology
- [ ] Have backup (PDF, offline version)
- [ ] Rehearse transitions

### During Presenting:
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Make eye contact
- Use presenter notes but don't read
- Pause for questions if appropriate
- Show passion for the problem

### After Presenting:
- Have materials ready to share
- Collect contact info
- Send follow-up within 24 hours
- Request feedback
