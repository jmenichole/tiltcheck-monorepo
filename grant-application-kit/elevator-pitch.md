## 2-Minute Pitch

**Use for:** Investor meetings, detailed grant apps, podcast intros

### Full 2-Minute Version:

**The Problem (30 seconds):**
Online casino Discord servers represent a $250B+ market with zero safety infrastructure. In these communities:
- Scam links spread rapidly, costing users millions
- Casinos manipulate bonuses with no accountability
- Users exhibit clear tilt behavior with no intervention
- There's no trust system to verify casinos or users
- Traditional gambling safety tools don't fit the culture

Most solutions are corporate, patronizing, or nonexistent.

**The Solution (45 seconds):**
TiltCheck is a modular ecosystem of Discord bots designed for casino communities.

Our core tools:
1. **SusLink** - Real-time link scanning and risk scoring
2. **TiltCheck Core** - AI-powered tilt detection with cooldown recommendations
3. **JustTheTip** - Non-custodial tipping and swapping via Solana
4. **Trust Engines** - Transparent scoring for casinos and users
5. **CollectClock** - Bonus tracking with pattern detection

Everything is Discord-first, non-custodial, and built on free-tier infrastructure. We use AI for pattern detection and predictions while respecting user autonomy.

**The Traction/Ask (30 seconds):**
I'm [Name], a solo female founder in the crypto gambling space. I've built complete technical documentation, identified key partnerships, and designed for free-tier survival using Cloudflare and Supabase.

We're seeking [amount] to launch the MVP covering SusLink, TiltCheck Core, and JustTheTip. First-year goals: 50+ Discord servers, 10K+ users, measurable scam reduction.

Long-term, we'll build revenue through survey routing, premium features, and network subscriptions - all while staying ethical and community-aligned.

**Why It Works (15 seconds):**
TiltCheck succeeds because it's built by someone who gets the culture. Not an outsider trying to "fix" gambling, but someone making it genuinely safer while respecting what makes these communities work.
